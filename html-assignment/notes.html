5 HTML tags I have learnt

<strong>
It takes one or more texts and make it bold or appear to be important texts. Ex: Awesome
<cite>
It is mainly used as a quote. At the end of the quote, you can have your name, URL, a copyright symbol...
<dt> & <dd>
A list that display the subjects with its description.
<sup>
They show texts superscripts. Ex:10th, 3rd
<q>
Adds a quotation to a sentence or a word. Ex: "I am a genius"
