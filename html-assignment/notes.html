5 HTML tags I have learnt

Strong
<strong> - It takes one or more texts and make it bold or appear to be important texts.
Citation
<cite> - It is mainly used as a quote. At the end of the quote, you can have your name, URL, a copyright symbol...
Discription list
<dt> - Subject that is being described. <dd>: Description of the subject.
Superscript
<sup> - The show superscripts in numbers. Ex:10th, 3rd
Quote
<q> - Adds a quotation to a sentence or a word. Ex: "I am a genius"
